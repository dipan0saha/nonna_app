## 1. COPPA Compliance

### 1.1 Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

This policy complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), which requires special protections for children under 13 years old.

**Key Points:**
- ‚úÖ We collect verifiable parental consent before collecting children's information
- ‚úÖ We provide notice about what information we collect
- ‚úÖ We give parents the right to review and delete their child's information
- ‚úÖ We do not condition participation on unnecessary data collection
- ‚úÖ We implement reasonable security measures to protect children's data

### 1.2 Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to information collected about:
- **Children under 13** (covered by COPPA in the U.S.)
- **Children under 16** (additional protections under GDPR in EU)
- **Any child whose parent creates a baby profile**

### 1.3 Parental Consent Requirement

**Before creating a baby profile, you must:**
- Be at least 18 years old
- Be the child's parent or legal guardian
- Affirm your authority to consent to data collection
- Accept this Children's Privacy Policy

We rely on parental consent as the legal basis for processing children's information.

### 5.1 Your Rights as a Parent/Guardian

Under COPPA and other laws, you have the right to:

#### 5.1.1 Review Your Child's Information
**How:** Through the baby profile in the app or by requesting a copy

#### 5.1.2 Correct Your Child's Information
**How:** Edit baby profile directly in the app  
**What You Can Edit:** Name, birth date, gender, photo  
**Timeline:** Immediate

#### 5.1.3 Delete Your Child's Information
**How:** Delete baby profile in app settings or email us  
**What Gets Deleted:** All profile data, photos, events, comments  
**Timeline:** Immediate from app, permanent deletion within 90 days (see Data Retention)

#### 5.1.4 Control Who Sees Your Child's Information
**How:** Invite/revoke access through profile settings  
**Granularity:** Control access per person  
**Timeline:** Immediate effect when you revoke access

#### 5.1.5 Export Your Child's Information
**How:** Request data export through app or email  
**Format:** JSON file (data) + ZIP file (photos/videos)  
**Timeline:** Within 30 days

#### 5.1.6 Withdraw Consent
**How:** Delete the baby profile  
**Effect:** All child's information deleted (subject to retention policy)  
**Recovery Period:** 90 days soft-delete period

## 6. Security and Protection

### 6.1 Security Measures for Children's Data

We implement enhanced security for children's information:

#### 6.1.1 Encryption
- **AES-256 encryption** for all data at rest (database, photos)
- **TLS 1.3 encryption** for all data in transit
- **Password hashing** (bcrypt) for parent accounts

#### 6.1.2 Access Controls
- **Row-Level Security (RLS):** Database-level access restrictions
- **Role-based permissions:** Only authorized family members can view
- **Private by default:** Profiles are never public
- **Invitation-only:** No one can find or search for children's profiles

#### 6.1.3 Automatic Privacy Protections
- **EXIF removal:** Location and metadata automatically removed from photos
- **No public search:** Children's profiles cannot be searched or discovered
- **No social features:** No friend requests, comments from strangers, or public interactions

### 6.2 Operational Security

**Employee Access:**
- Employees do NOT have access to children's information
- Access granted only on need-to-know basis (e.g., customer support with parent consent)
- All access logged and audited

**Third-Party Security:**
- All vendors undergo security assessment
- Data Processing Agreements require COPPA compliance
- Regular security audits

**Incident Response:**
- Monitoring for unauthorized access
- Incident response plan for data breaches
- Parent notification within 72 hours of confirmed breach

### 6.3 What Parents Can Do

You can enhance your child's safety by:
- **Using a strong password** for your account
- **Enabling two-factor authentication** (when available)
- **Only inviting trusted family members**
- **Reviewing who has access** to profiles regularly
- **Being cautious about sharing** on other platforms
- **Educating family members** about not re-sharing photos

## 7. Data Retention for Children's Information

### 7.1 How Long We Keep Children's Information

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Active profile data** | While profile exists |
| **Deleted profile** | 90-day grace period, then permanent deletion |
| **Individual photos** | 30-day grace period after deletion |
| **Backups** | Up to 90 days after deletion from production |
| **Parental consent logs** | 7 years after profile deletion (legal compliance) |

### 7.2 Automatic Deletion

If the parent account is inactive for 3 years:
1. Warning email sent at 2.5 years
2. Final warning at 3 years
3. Automatic deletion after 90-day grace period

### 7.3 Parental Deletion Rights

You can delete your child's profile at any time:
- **Immediate effect:** Profile disabled immediately
- **Grace period:** 90 days to recover (contact support)
- **Permanent deletion:** After 90 days, unrecoverable
- **Backup removal:** Deleted from all backups within 90 days

For more details, see our [Data Retention Policy](DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md).
