## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Our Commitment to Privacy

At Nonna App, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices.

**Our Privacy Principles:**
- **Privacy by Design:** Privacy is built into every feature from the ground up
- **Data Minimization:** We only collect what we need to provide the Service
- **No Ads or Tracking:** We do not use advertising networks or analytics tracking
- **No Data Selling:** We will never sell your data to third parties
- **Your Control:** You decide who sees your content and can delete it anytime
- **Transparency:** We are open about what data we collect and how we use it

### 1.2 Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Nonna App, including:
- Account holders (parents/guardians)
- Family members with access to profiles
- Visitors to our website (if applicable)

For information specific to children's data, please see Section 8 and our [Children's Privacy Policy](CHILDRENS_PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

### 1.3 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:
- **Consent:** You have given us clear consent to process your data
- **Contract Performance:** Processing is necessary to provide the Service
- **Legal Obligations:** We must process data to comply with laws
- **Legitimate Interests:** We have legitimate business interests that do not override your privacy rights

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide to Us

#### 2.1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (required for login and communication)
- **Password** (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text)
- **Full name** (optional, for personalization)
- **Profile photo** (optional)
- **Phone number** (optional, for account recovery)

#### 2.1.2 Baby/Child Profile Information
When you create a baby profile, we collect:
- **Baby's name** (first name, middle name, last name)
- **Birth date** (for age calculation and milestones)
- **Gender** (optional)
- **Profile photo** (optional)
- **Relationship** (your relationship to the child)

**Parental Consent:** By creating a baby profile, you affirm that you are the parent or legal guardian and consent to the collection and processing of the child's information as described in this policy.

#### 2.1.3 Content You Share
- **Photos and videos** (shared within family profiles)
- **Comments and messages** (on photos, events, or posts)
- **Event details** (family events, appointments, milestones)
- **Registry items** (baby registry wish lists)
- **Calendar entries** (family calendar events)

#### 2.1.4 Family Network Information
- **Follower relationships** (who you invite to follow your profile)
- **Role assignments** (owner vs. follower permissions)
- **Invitation emails** (to invite family members)

#### 2.2.1 Technical Information
- **Device information** (device type, operating system, app version)
- **IP address** (for security and fraud prevention)
- **Access logs** (login times, actions performed for security monitoring)
- **Session data** (authentication tokens, session duration)

#### 2.2.2 Usage Information
We collect minimal usage information:
- **Feature usage** (which features you use to improve the Service)
- **Error logs** (crash reports to fix bugs)
- **Performance data** (load times, upload speeds to optimize performance)

**Important:** We do NOT use third-party analytics tools. All usage data is collected first-party and used solely to improve the Service.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we DON'T collect:
- ❌ **Browsing history** outside the app
- ❌ **Location data** (GPS, geolocation)
- ❌ **Contacts** from your device

- ❌ **Behavioral profiling data**
- ❌ **Data for advertising purposes**

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

We take data security seriously and implement industry-leading security measures:

#### 5.1.1 Encryption
- **At Rest:** AES-256 encryption for all data stored in the database and file storage
- **In Transit:** TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
- **Password Storage:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)

#### 5.1.2 Access Controls
- **Row-Level Security (RLS):** Database-level access controls ensure users can only access their authorized data
- **JWT Authentication:** Secure token-based authentication
- **Role-Based Permissions:** Owners and followers have different permission levels
- **Session Management:** Automatic session expiration and secure session handling

#### 5.1.3 Infrastructure Security
- **SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure** (via Supabase)
- **ISO 27001 certified data centers**
- **Regular security audits and penetration testing**
- **Automated backups** with encryption
- **DDoS protection and network security**

#### 5.1.4 Operational Security
- **Access logging:** All data access is logged for security monitoring
- **Incident response plan:** Procedures for detecting and responding to security incidents
- **Employee training:** Staff trained on data protection and security best practices
- **Principle of least privilege:** Employees have minimal access necessary for their role

### 5.2 Your Security Responsibilities

You can help protect your account by:
- **Using a strong, unique password**
- **Never sharing your password** with anyone
- **Enabling two-factor authentication** (when available)
- **Logging out on shared devices**
- **Reviewing account activity** regularly
- **Reporting suspicious activity** immediately

### 5.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that may affect your rights and freedoms, we will:
- **Notify you within 72 hours** (as required by GDPR)
- **Explain what happened** and what data was affected
- **Describe steps we're taking** to mitigate the breach
- **Provide guidance** on protecting yourself
- **Notify relevant authorities** as required by law

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

### 7.1 Rights for All Users

Regardless of your location, you have the following rights:

#### 7.1.1 Access Your Data
- **Right to know:** What personal data we have about you
- **Right to review:** Access your data through the app or by requesting a copy

#### 7.1.2 Correct Your Data
- **Right to correction:** Fix inaccurate or incomplete data
- **How to exercise:** Update information directly in the app

#### 7.1.3 Delete Your Data
- **Right to deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **How to exercise:** Use in-app deletion features or contact us

#### 7.1.4 Export Your Data
- **Right to portability:** Receive a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Format:** JSON file with all your data, plus ZIP archive of photos
- **How to exercise:** Request data export through app settings or contact us

#### 7.1.5 Opt-Out
- **Right to opt-out:** Of non-essential communications
- **How to exercise:** App settings or email unsubscribe links

### 7.2 Additional Rights for EU/EEA Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights:

#### 7.2.1 Right to Object
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Object to direct marketing (including profiling)

#### 7.2.2 Right to Restrict Processing
- Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

#### 7.2.3 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing)

#### 7.2.4 Right to Lodge a Complaint
- File a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority
- **EU DPA List:** [https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en](https://edpb.europa.eu/about-edpb/board/members_en)

#### 7.2.5 Automated Decision-Making
- Right not to be subject to solely automated decisions with legal effects
- **Note:** Nonna App does not use automated decision-making or profiling

### 7.3 Additional Rights for California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

#### 7.3.1 Right to Know
- What personal information we collect, use, and disclose

#### 7.3.2 Right to Delete
- Request deletion of your personal information

#### 7.3.3 Right to Opt-Out
- **Note:** We do NOT sell personal information, so there is nothing to opt out of

#### 7.3.4 Right to Non-Discrimination
- We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

#### 7.3.5 Shine the Light
- California residents can request information about data shared with third parties for marketing (we do not share for marketing)

### 7.4 How to Exercise Your Rights

**In-App:**
- Access, correct, or delete data through app settings
- Request data export through privacy dashboard

**By Email:**
- Send requests to [privacy@nonnaapp.example.com]
- We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)

**Verification:**
- We may ask for verification to protect your privacy
- We will confirm your identity before fulfilling requests
