## 1. Market Landscape

### 1.1 Market Overview

**Market Fragmentation:**

The family sharing and baby tracking app market is highly fragmented with three distinct categories:

| Category | Focus | Leading Apps | Market Share |
|----------|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **Photo Sharing** | Family photo/video sharing | FamilyAlbum, Tinybeans, 23snaps | 60% |
